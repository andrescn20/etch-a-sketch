Etch-a-sketch (WORK IN PROGRESS)

Instructions

  1. Enter your desired grid size (max = 100).
  2. Press 'Generate' to create a blanck grid.
  3. Pass the mouse over the grid to paint
  4. Reset:Press 'Generate' again to clear the Grid. Entering a new number will generate a new clean grid.
  • Erase: Press to activate eraser (Button is white when engaged)
  • Color Theme: Press to change between black and colorful modes.
